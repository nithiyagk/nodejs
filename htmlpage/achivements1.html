ACHIVEMENTS OF MISSLE MAN OF INDIA


1. Space and Defense Career:

Dr. Kalam played a crucial role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. He worked as the project director for the development of India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III) at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

2. Integrated Guided Missile Development Program (IGMDP):

Dr. Kalam was the chief architect of the IGMDP, which resulted in the development of ballistic missiles like Agni and Prithvi. He played a pivotal role in the successful testing of these missiles, showcasing India's self-reliance in defense technology.

3.Bharat Ratna :

In 1997, Dr. Abdul Kalam was honored with the Padma Bhushan, and in 1998, he received the Padma Vibhushan, two of India's highest civilian awards. In 2002, he became the 11th President of India and received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 1997.

4.Vision 2020 :

Dr. Kalam had a vision for India to become a developed nation by the year 2020. He advocated for sustainable economic growth, technological advancement, and societal development.

5.Educational Initiatives :

After his presidential term, Dr. Kalam focused on education and youth development. He interacted extensively with students and inspired them to pursue science and technology.
